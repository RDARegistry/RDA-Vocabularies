Map from deprecated to published RDA Layout concepts

The map relates the deprecated URIs in the deprecated published and unpublished RDA Layout of ... value vocabularies to the published URIs for the consolidated RDA Layout value vocabulary.

This display of the map is in a terse triple language (ttl) serialization.

@prefix owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#> .
@prefix layout: <http://rdaregistry.info/termList/layout/> .
@prefix layoutCartoImage: <http://rdaregistry.info/termList/layoutCartoImage/> .
@prefix layoutTacMusic: <http://rdaregistry.info/termList/layoutTacMusic/> .
