Turtle (text/turtle)

The map consists of mappings that can be combined with the Map from unconstrained ISBD properties to unconstrained properties derived from RDA to entail property equivalence in OWL.

For example:

# from this map:
rdau:P60050 rdfs:subPropertyOf isbdu:P1003 .
# from map of unconstrained ISBD to unconstrained RDA:
isbdu:P1003 rdfs:subPropertyOf rdau:P60050 .
# entails:
rdau:P60050 owl:equivalentProperty isbdu:P1003 .

The map is given in a terse triple language (ttl) serialization.
