This section contains examples of sets of data using the RDA vocabularies to describe a single resource.

These examples are illustrative, and no guarantee is given that they are accurate.

The examples are also used for RDA Toolkit examples r-balls. Variations in the RDF representations are intentional and are compatible with RDA guidance and instructions.
